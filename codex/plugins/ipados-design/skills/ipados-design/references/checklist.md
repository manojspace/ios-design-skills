# iPadOS Final Checklist

## Product Parity

- Same identity as the iPhone app.
- Same data state quality: content/loading/empty/error/stale.
- Same terminology and formatting.
- Same signature product moment adapted to iPad.

## iPad Quality

- Layout adapts across compact, regular, portrait, landscape, and large windows.
- No stretched iPhone UI.
- Sidebar/content/detail/inspector choices match the workflow.
- Pointer, keyboard, touch, and Pencil expectations are considered.
- Menu bar, toolbar, context menu, and shortcuts expose repeated actions.
- Popovers replace oversized modals for lightweight choices.

## Visual

- Light and dark screenshots inspected.
- Large canvas has useful context, not decoration.
- Text lines are readable and not overly wide.
- Panels, separators, and selected states remain visible.

## Accessibility

- Dynamic Type large and accessibility sizes checked.
- Increased contrast checked.
- Keyboard focus and selection are visible.
- Hit targets are at least 44x44pt.
- Reduce Motion is respected.

## Verification

- iPad screenshot matrix was run when possible.
- Any unverified device/window/orientation coverage is stated.
